About Aditya Singh

Aditya Singh is a new-age Indian political strategist who uniquely merges the analytical lens of a journalist with the precision of a campaign architect. As Co-Founder and Director of V SCAN Alliance (Vikas Strategic Citizens Accountability Network), he leads a youth-driven movement that champions transparency, accountability, and people-first politics across India.

With over 3 years of intensive on-ground field experience, Aditya has shaped winning narratives and data-led digital campaigns for political parties and candidates pan-India. His defining expertise lies in crafting strategies that not only trend online but deeply resonate offline — a rare synthesis of media insight, social influence, and grassroots citizen engagement into one powerful formula.

A postgraduate in Journalism from the prestigious Delhi University with an advanced specialization, Aditya brings academic rigor to every campaign he architects. Before co-founding V SCAN Alliance, he worked with leading political consulting firms where he spearheaded public outreach, content strategy, and influencer-driven political storytelling.

Smart, strategic, and deeply rooted in ground reality, Aditya Singh represents the next generation of Indian political minds — committed to democratizing political consulting and making governance truly responsive to citizens.
