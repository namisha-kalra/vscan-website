Aditya Singh

Co-Founder & Director, V SCAN ALLIANCE

“Strategizing Governance. Empowering Democracy.”

Aditya Singh is a new-age political strategist who merges the lens of a journalist with the precision of a campaign architect. As Co-Founder of V SCAN Alliance (Vikas Strategic Citizens Accountability Network), he leads a youth-driven movement that champions transparency, accountability, and people-first politics.

With over 3 years of on-ground experience, Aditya has shaped winning narratives and data-led digital campaigns for political parties and candidates across India. His expertise lies in crafting strategies that don’t just trend online but resonate offline — blending media insight, social influence, and citizen engagement into one impactful formula.

A journalism postgraduate from Delhi University with advanced specialization, Aditya has worked with leading political consulting firms where he spearheaded public outreach, content strategy, and influencer-driven storytelling.

Smart, strategic, and rooted in reality — Aditya Singh represents the next generation of political minds committed to democratizing political consulting and making governance truly responsive.
