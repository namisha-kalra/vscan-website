Runny Tomar

Co-Founder & Director, V SCAN ALLIANCE

“Strategizing Governance. Empowering Democracy.”

Runny Tomar is a grassroots strategist and policy thinker, serving as Co-Founder of V SCAN Alliance (Vikas Strategic Citizens Accountability Network) — a youth-led platform advancing inclusive, accountable politics across India.

Armed with a postgraduate degree in Gender Studies from Jamia Millia Islamia and a strong foundation in History, Runny brings a rare blend of academic depth and real-world political insight. His approach combines historical context with present-day social realities to design campaigns that are inclusive, people-centric, and socially aware.

Having worked with both leading political consultancies and community-driven NGOs, Runny specializes in community research, gender-sensitive communication, and policy outreach. His on-ground understanding makes him a vital force in driving V SCAN’s commitment to equitable governance and fair political participation.

Progressive, perceptive, and people-first — Runny Tomar is shaping a new political narrative where inclusion meets strategy.
