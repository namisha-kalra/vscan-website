Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about V SCAN Alliance and our services

🏢 About V SCAN
What is V SCAN Alliance?
+

V SCAN Alliance (Vikas Strategic Citizens Accountability Network) is India's premier political & policy consultancy firm, founded by Vikas Kumar Choudhary. We specialize in ethical, data-driven political consulting — helping leaders win elections and govern effectively across India.

Where is V SCAN based and where do you operate?
+

V SCAN is headquartered in Shahdara, Delhi (National Capital). We currently operate across 11 states — Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Punjab, Uttarakhand, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, Odisha, and Maharashtra — with plans to expand further.

How long has V SCAN been operating?
+

V SCAN has been operating for 7+ years with consistent excellence in political consulting. Since establishment, we have managed 100+ campaigns, trained 500+ leaders, and maintained an active presence across 11 states.

What makes V SCAN different from other political consultancies?
+

V SCAN is built on three pillars: Ethics, Data, and Governance. We only work with leaders genuinely committed to public service. We use real booth-level data — not assumptions. And we work beyond election day into governance delivery, ensuring promises are kept.

🛠️ Services & Process
What services does V SCAN provide?
+

We offer 6 core services: (1) Electoral Strategy & Management, (2) Voter Research & Data Analytics, (3) Digital & Media Strategy, (4) Governance & Policy Advisory, (5) Political Intelligence Unit, and (6) Leadership & Capacity Building. We also create custom packages. See full details →

How does the engagement process work?
+

Our process: 1) Discovery call to understand your requirements, 2) Constituency research & voter data analysis, 3) Custom strategy blueprint, 4) Campaign execution with real-time war room, 5) Post-election governance advisory. We work as your dedicated campaign partner throughout.

Do you work only before elections or also after?
+

Both! V SCAN works across the full political lifecycle — from pre-election strategy to post-election governance. We help leaders not just win elections but govern responsibly and deliver on their promises to voters.

Do you work with all political parties?
+

V SCAN is a non-partisan consultancy. We work with individuals and parties who are genuinely committed to accountable, citizen-first governance — regardless of political affiliation. Our only criterion is ethical commitment to public service.

💰 Partnership & Pricing
How do I partner with V SCAN?
+

Simply contact us with your requirements, constituency details, and timeline. Our team will schedule a discovery call, assess your needs, and propose a customized engagement plan tailored to your political goals.

What is the typical timeline for engagement?
+

For elections, we recommend engaging at least 6-12 months before polling. However, we can onboard clients at any stage of the campaign cycle with rapid deployment capabilities. For governance advisory, engagement can begin immediately post-election.

Do you handle individual candidates or only parties?
+

We work with both individual candidates (MLA, MP, local body) and political parties at state and national level. We also work with independent candidates who demonstrate strong public service commitment.
