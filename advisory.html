Mr. K.P. Malik

Senior Journalist & Political Editor

Mr. K.P. Malik is a highly respected senior journalist with over 30 years of rich experience in active journalism, bringing unmatched credibility and insight into the socio-political and media landscape of India. Currently serving as Political Editor at Dainik Bhaskar, he also holds key leadership roles as Vice President of the Delhi Journalists Association (DJA - NUK India), Executive Member of the Press Association of India, and a Member of the Press Council of India (PCI). He has previously served as the Secretary General of the Delhi Journalists Association and the Journalist Welfare Association, advocating for the rights and welfare of media professionals.

Throughout his distinguished career, Mr. Malik has worked with some of the most reputed media houses including Doordarshan, BBC, CNBC, Zee News, Sahara Samay, and Hindustan Times. He has covered numerous high-profile events including Prime Ministers‚Äô meetings, Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha sessions, and several national and international summits across the country. One of the defining moments of his career was his historic live coverage of the terrorist attack on the Indian Parliament in December 2001, a testament to his courage and commitment to journalism.

Known for his unwavering dedication to public interest, Mr. Malik has consistently raised the voices of the poor and farmers before the government, becoming a powerful bridge between policy-makers and the grassroots. As an Advisory Committee Member at V SCAN Alliance, his decades of frontline journalistic experience contribute invaluable socio-political insight and policy evaluation expertise, offering a futuristic and people-centric vision that helps steer the organization toward impactful and accountable governance.

Prof. (Dr.) Santosh Kumar Karn

Advisory Committee Member

Prof. (Dr.) Santosh Kumar Karn is a distinguished academician, research leader, and global science advisor, currently serving as Director and Professor at the Chandras Centre for Advanced Research and Development (CCARD), Rohini, New Delhi, and as Academic Advisor to the Chancellor at the International University, Dimapur (New Delhi Office).

He has held several senior academic and leadership positions, including Professor associated with the Founder Chancellor at IGTMS University, SBSR Professor at Sharda University (Greater Noida), Professor and Dean (Examinations), and Head of the Department of Science and Humanities at PCCS, Greater Noida.

Prof. Karn has been an active member of key academic and scientific bodies such as the Board of Studies (Physical Sciences) of former Mahamaya Technical University and Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), the Science Advisory Board (USA), and the Board of Associates of ECT, Trento, Italy. His international academic exposure includes appointments in the USA, Italy, and Spain, along with research associations with INSA and HRI in India.

A recipient of prestigious awards and research grants from DST, AICTE, INSA, CSIR, Delhi University, AKTU, and other leading institutions, he brings deep expertise in research-driven policy, academic governance, and institutional development.

As an Advisory Committee Member of V SCAN Alliance, he contributes strategic guidance to strengthen evidence-based public policy, governance evaluation, and knowledge-led decision-making.
