Mr. K.P. Malik

Senior Journalist & Political Editor

Mr. K.P. Malik is a highly respected senior journalist with over 30 years of rich experience in active journalism, bringing unmatched credibility and insight into the socio-political and media landscape of India. Currently serving as Political Editor at Dainik Bhaskar, he also holds key leadership roles as Vice President of the Delhi Journalists Association (DJA - NUK India), Executive Member of the Press Association of India, and a Member of the Press Council of India (PCI). He has previously served as the Secretary General of the Delhi Journalists Association and the Journalist Welfare Association, advocating for the rights and welfare of media professionals.

Throughout his distinguished career, Mr. Malik has worked with some of the most reputed media houses including Doordarshan, BBC, CNBC, Zee News, Sahara Samay, and Hindustan Times. He has covered numerous high-profile events including Prime Ministers’ meetings, Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha sessions, and several national and international summits across the country. One of the defining moments of his career was his historic live coverage of the terrorist attack on the Indian Parliament in December 2001, a testament to his courage and commitment to journalism.

Known for his unwavering dedication to public interest, Mr. Malik has consistently raised the voices of the poor and farmers before the government, becoming a powerful bridge between policy-makers and the grassroots. As an Advisory Committee Member at V SCAN Alliance, his decades of frontline journalistic experience contribute invaluable socio-political insight and policy evaluation expertise, offering a futuristic and people-centric vision that helps steer the organization toward impactful and accountable governance.

Dr. Gyanendra Kumar Attri

Environmental Sustainability Expert

Dr. Gyanendra Kumar Attri is one of India’s leading experts in environmental sustainability, waste management, and life cycle assessment (LCA). A certified Green Building Consultant and renowned scholar, he brings a rare combination of academic excellence and strategic foresight to sustainable development. Dr. Attri holds a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from Jaypee University, an MBA from Jaypee Business School, an M.Tech from Noida International University, and a Ph.D. with distinction from MNIT Jaipur in Sustainable Materials and Construction. He is the Founder & CEO of Sustainability Solutions and Director at GPV Infra Solutions, where he leads innovative, green infrastructure and policy-driven initiatives.

With a rich career that includes academic roles as Professor at Noida International University and Dronacharya Group of Institutions, Dr. Attri has also held key leadership positions in the development and construction sectors, such as Senior Manager at Development Alternatives (International Division) and Jaypee Construction. His expertise is recognized across several high-level government and non-government platforms including the Y20 India Program Taskforce, Atal Innovation Mission, Institute of Lean Construction Excellence (ILCE), IUCN, Global Sustainability Forum Network (GSFN), Wadhwani Foundation, CSO Foundation, and the Steering Committee hosted by the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).

As a member of the Advisory Committee at V SCAN Alliance, Dr. Attri plays a vital role in shaping the organization’s strategic direction. His vast experience and insights provide a futuristic and sustainability-driven vision, aligning political consulting and public policy with the principles of accountability, innovation, and environmental stewardship.
