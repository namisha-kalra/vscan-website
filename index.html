Vikas Kumar Choudhary, born in Baghpat, Uttar Pradesh, India, is a prominent political strategist, governance thinker, motivational speaker, and the Founder & CEO of V SCAN Alliance (Vikas Strategic Citizens Accountability Network) — a next-generation political consulting and policy firm committed to accountable governance and citizen-first leadership across India.

With over 7 years of extensive experience and a proven track record of guiding 50+ political leaders and parties, Vikas has established himself as one of India's most trusted voices in ethical and data-driven political consulting. His pan-India reach and deep understanding of electoral dynamics have helped candidates and parties achieve decisive victories across the country.

A landmark chapter in Vikas's career was his role as Media & PR Strategist with the State Election Commission, Delhi — where he led a senior managerial team to create large-scale voter awareness campaigns and was directly responsible for increasing voter polling percentages across the capital. He managed PR operations and coordinated with all key stakeholders at the highest levels of governance.

Academically trained in Journalism and Public Administration, Vikas has also served as Senior Consultant at two leading MNCs — Hendry Harbin and Data Trend — managing national and international client networks and driving revenue through strategic sales and PR operations, working alongside Chetan Bhagat as brand ambassador. He has also been selected for 5+ international conferences on governance, health, and poverty — representing India on global platforms including India Model United Nations and India Social Talks — and has delivered 90+ motivational talks inspiring the next generation of leaders.
