About Vikas Kumar Choudhary

Vikas Kumar Choudhary is a prominent Indian political strategist, governance thinker, and the Founder & CEO of V SCAN Alliance (Vikas Strategic Citizens Accountability Network) — a next-generation political consulting and policy firm committed to accountable governance and citizen-first leadership across India.

With extensive field experience spanning more than 10 Indian states, Vikas has emerged as one of India's distinguished voices in ethical and data-driven political consulting. He has successfully guided political parties and candidates to electoral victory by crafting strategic, research-backed, and value-driven campaigns — transforming political vision into public trust and popular mandate.

His leadership philosophy uniquely bridges grassroots realities with high-level strategic decision-making. Academically trained in Journalism and Public Administration, Vikas combines sharp communication instincts with deep policy understanding — a rare combination that enables him to craft narratives that resonate with both voters and governance frameworks.

Before founding V SCAN Alliance, Vikas held senior roles in leading political consultancies, where he shaped voter outreach programs and aligned campaign strategies with genuine governance objectives. His career represents a consistent mission: to make democracy more accountable, campaigns more honest, and governance more citizen-centric.
